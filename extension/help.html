üéØ What MindFlow Does

MindFlow transforms your casual counseling notes into professional, Minnesota 245G-compliant progress notes.

Example Transformation

Your Input: Client seemed anxious today, about 7 out of 10. Been clean for 30 days. We worked on breathing exercises. Gave homework to write down triggers. MindFlow Output: SERVICE PROVIDED: Provided 50-minute individual substance use disorder counseling session at ASAM Level 2.1. CLIENT RESPONSE: Client presented with anxiety symptoms, self-reporting 7/10 severity. Reported 30 days abstinent from substances. INTERVENTIONS: Implemented anxiety management interventions including breathing exercises addressing Dimension 3 (Emotional/Behavioral). PROGRESS: Client demonstrating progress toward Goal #1 with 30 days abstinent. PLAN: Continue weekly sessions. Client to complete trigger identification worksheet.

üîß Troubleshooting

Purple Dot Not Appearing?

  • Make sure the extension is enabled in Chrome
  • Refresh the page you're testing on
  • Try the included test page first
  • Check that textareas are large enough (minimum 200x60 pixels)

Enhancement Not Working?

  • Make sure there's text in the textarea before clicking enhance
  • Check the browser console for error messages (F12 ‚Üí Console)
  • Try with the provided test cases first
  • Ensure you're not in an incognito window

Extension Status Check

To verify MindFlow is working:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Find "MindFlow - Clinical Note Enhancement"
  3. Make sure it's enabled (toggle switch is blue)
  4. Click "Details" and check for any errors

üéì Minnesota 245G Compliance

MindFlow ensures all notes meet Minnesota 245G requirements:

  • 5 Required Sections: Service Provided, Client Response, Interventions, Progress, Plan
  • ASAM Dimensions: Automatic reference to appropriate dimensions 1-6
  • Treatment Goals: Progress section references specific goals
  • Measurable Outcomes: Includes numbers, dates, and objective measures
  • Professional Language: Converts casual terms to clinical terminology
